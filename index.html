Shri G.S. Matharoo, Chairman, PRUDENCE School, Ashok Vihar, Aligarh College of Engineering and Technology provides high quality training in technical and management education leading to B.Tech, MCA, MBA, BBA and BCA under the guidance of highly qualified and motivated faculty, which includes highly experienced professors of repute.

For the past 19 years, this institute has implemented contemporary methods by careful planning and eventually producing resourceful professionals acquiring leadership qualities in technology and engineering.

The various courses permit students to obtain knowledge and improve their ability in planning and designing giving rise to creative innovators that will bring about a positive impact on their environment and global situations. The development of diverse competencies in students, aids in shaping a unique identity of their own providing a clearer pathway for their career. The skills and knowledge they will acquire here will establish them as assets for any organisation, institute or job they apply to in the future.

ACET encourages an academic environment for its students as well as faculty members. Students are provided with an excellent teacher-student learning process system allowing for better communication and gaining relevant information. The choice of faculty members have been carefully selected based on their experience and suitability of the subject, their drive in research and positive learning environment for students.

ACET has ambitious plans for the future, to further advance and broaden its horizon, be it in providing better infrastructural facilities, increase in extra-curricular activities allowing for an overall enriching learning experience for the students and be regarded amongst the top academic institutes in the field of Engineering and Technology. This institute aspires to form students where they have a compassion, responsibility and commitment to improve the society and environment at large.

ACET warmly welcomes you to join our community that provides you with an enriching environment to further enhance your creative skills, gain the relevant information for a professional education and to experience a challenging yet fruitful road for a successful career
